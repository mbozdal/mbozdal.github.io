## Project Overview

**HI-ACT (Hydrogen Integration for Accelerated Energy Transitions)** is a UK-wide research hub uniting academic institutions, industry stakeholders, government agencies, and the public to address key challenges in the integration of hydrogen and alternative liquid fuels (HALF). The hub comprises ten leading UK universities and over forty project partners, fostering interdisciplinary collaboration across research, innovation, and policy domains.

This project contributes to **Work Package 3 (Cyber-Physical Industry)** and focuses on developing secure, resilient, and efficient architectures for hydrogen integration across industrial and energy sectors.
