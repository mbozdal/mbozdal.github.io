## Project Overview

IoT-Based Smart Charging Management for Electric Vehicles is a research project aimed at optimizing
 the charging processes of electric vehicles (EVs) in a sustainable and grid-friendly manner.
 The rapid growth in EV adoption poses challenges for low-voltage distribution grids,
 especially during peak demand hours. This project proposes an intelligent charging management system
 that dynamically balances user preferences, grid conditions, and renewable energy availability.

The system integrates Internet of Things (IoT) devices, machine learning algorithms, and real-time energy
 data to schedule charging sessions in a way that supports energy efficiency, cost savings, and grid stability.


## Project Objectives

1. Reduce peak load on low-voltage distribution networks by optimizing EV charging schedules
2. Maximize the use of renewable energy in EV charging
3. Build a modular, scalable, and user-centric smart charging platform for real-world deployment
